

1 Scope (Requirements)
- login admin account
A. Home (Dashboard)

Show KPIs: Today & This Month (Sales, Purchases, Gross Profit), Stock Value, Top 5 items sold, Low-stock alerts.
Quick links: New Sale, New Purchase, Manage Items, Reports.

B. Contacts

Two types: Customers and Suppliers.
CRUD contacts (name, phone, email, address, note, active).
Use customer in Sales; supplier in Purchases.

C. Items (Products + Stock)

CRUD items: name, SKU, category (optional), cost price, sell price, stock on hand, reorder level.
Stock updates:

Purchase increases stock at item’s cost.
Sale decreases stock at item’s sell.


Prevent sale if stock would go negative (warn/allow toggle in Settings).

D. Purchase Management

Create Purchase: supplier, date, line items (item, qty, unit cost), total.
Update stock on save. Allow delete (reverse stock).
List & filter purchases (date, supplier, item).

E. Sales (existing)

Add customer to sale (optional).
Validate stock (based on Settings).
Delete sale reverses stock.

F. Reports

Item Sales Report: by date range; totals qty, revenue by item.
Item Purchase Report: by date range; totals qty, cost by item.
Profit & Loss: (Sales Revenue − COGS − Discounts [if any]; we’ll skip discounts now).

COGS uses moving average cost or simple cost (we will use Average Cost simplified).


Stock Report: current stock, stock value (qty × cost), low-stock items.
Export to CSV (optional add-on).
